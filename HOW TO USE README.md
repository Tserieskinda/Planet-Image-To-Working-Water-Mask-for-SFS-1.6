What this achieves- You know that one exoplanet that you're TRYING to make the watermask for
for your New custom system? Well you don't have to spend 15 hours on it now, because this tool makes it
so much easier and efficient.

How to use-
Download the HTML and Open in Browser 
Get the planet Texture, make sure it's perfectly cropped into a square around the planet.
Upload it into the program 
Select Water button from bottom and click on the water which in on your planet texture,
Remember to click on different shades of water in your texture, the more accurate result will be.
Select land from the bottom and do the same, select different shades and colours of land (may need lots of samples for complicated textures)
After you've put a bunch of land and water samples, click generate.
the image might look foggy but, Turn down all these sliders- Contrast,Depth,Spread,Coast To 0 to get a sharp black and white watermask.
You can click on "Terrain circle" button to get an outline of a circle, this will now allow you to select the black/white pen from bottom
and now you can add/remove finishing touches manually, and the outline allows you to see the circumference of the planet.
You can also adjust the pen settings such as Size and opacity.
Download and use in your System When you're happy with the result!
